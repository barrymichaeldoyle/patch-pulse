# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 10, 2026**

PatchPulse ("the app") is a Slack application that monitors npm package versions and sends notifications to your Slack workspace.

## What we collect

- **Workspace information** — Slack team ID and team name, used to identify your workspace.
- **Bot access token** — stored securely to send messages on behalf of the app.
- **Package names** — the npm packages you choose to track.
- **Slack user IDs and channel IDs** — used to route notifications to the correct destination (DM or channel).

## What we do not collect

- We do not collect message content from your Slack workspace.
- We do not collect personal information beyond what Slack provides during OAuth installation.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Data storage

All data is stored in [Convex](https://convex.dev), a secure cloud database. Data is associated with your Slack workspace and is only used to deliver package update notifications.

## Data deletion

To remove your workspace's data, uninstall PatchPulse from your Slack workspace. You can also open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/barrymichaeldoyle/patch-pulse/issues) to request manual deletion.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, open an issue at [github.com/barrymichaeldoyle/patch-pulse](https://github.com/barrymichaeldoyle/patch-pulse/issues).
